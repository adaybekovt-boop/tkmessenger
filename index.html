Privacy Policy

Zero server storage: Orbits does not store your messages, keys, or media on centralized servers.

Local control: Chat history is stored locally on your device.

Responsibility: If you lose your password, access cannot be restored because there is no centralized recovery registry.

Technical logs: Only technical metadata is used to establish P2P connections (node ID, NAT type).

Screen Privacy: The app uses anti-capture protection where supported by the OS.

By continuing, you confirm that you understand the risks and accept the terms of use.
